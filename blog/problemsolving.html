Problem Solving

Everyone has had those times in their lives, where they're up against challenges and problems. Me personally, i've decided to go full send, and do this deving thing. At times i think this is a clear example of my insanity, because everything seems like a struggle.

Learning new skills, in particular coding can prove frustrating. Even the simplest of problems can prove the most taxing. And all through this sprint in particular, i have foundmyself faced with challenges. What i perceive to be relatively straight forward, makes me scratch my head longer than i care to admit. But its also been a good lesson on determination, perservance and problem solving.

The first obstance I had, was with the FizzBuzz problem. It seemed simple enough write a function to returns "Fizz" for multiples of 3, "Buzz" for multiples of 5, and "FizzBuzz" where the number was divisible by both 3 and 5.

I had to use several problem solving techniques, the one i utilised the most was "Rubber Ducky" in this case i talked out loud to my trusty peace lily. Things seem to make more sense when i say it out loud than when i repeat it over in my head. I also utilised, console.logging to debug my errors, and i found Pseudocoding to be really good as well. Though i find i prefer to write my pseudocode manually on a peice of paper than in to the terminal itself.

I'm still not good at asking for help, i really need to work on this. I've always been a self directed learner. I try and use google assistance, to deconstruct my code to define where my errors might be in it, rather than using it for the answer.

How confident do i feel? A little bit more every day. It comes in waves, there are glory moments and then face palm moments. But it all comes back to perservance.
