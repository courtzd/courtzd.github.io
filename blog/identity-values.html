Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

Choosing to quit my job, because the policies did not align with my personal values. This was a very personal ethical decision i had to make for myself. It took me months. The biggest fear was financial instablity. But i knew if i remained I would not have done my job to the best of my ability which is equally unfair to my colleagues. Do i regret quitting. No. I feel happier and am now doing things that interest me. I feel far more fulfilled and still back my decision, regardless of the financial impact.
